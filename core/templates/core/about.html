About Sesika

Sesika is a peer-to-peer barter marketplace built for Ghana. We believe in the power of the swap — trading what you have for what you need, without cash always having to be the middleman.

Whether you have a spare phone, handmade crafts, or professional skills to offer, Sesika connects you with traders across Ghana who are looking for exactly what you have.

Add cash top-ups when values don't perfectly match. Rent items instead of buying. List your wishlist and get notified the moment something you want appears — our AI matching engine works around the clock to connect you.

How it works
📋
Build your wishlist
Tell us what you want and we'll notify you the moment a match appears.
📦
List what you have
Post items you want to trade, rent out, or services you can offer.
🤝
Make or receive an offer
Negotiate directly with traders. Add a cash top-up or counter-offer to close the deal.
💬
Connect on WhatsApp
Once an offer is accepted, contact details are revealed so you can arrange the swap.
