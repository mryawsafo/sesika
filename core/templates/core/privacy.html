Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect your phone number when you register, and any profile information you choose to add (name, location, bio). When you post a listing, we store the listing content and photos you upload. We also log basic device and session information for security purposes.

If you opt in to student verification, we collect and store your university email address solely for verification purposes.

2. How we use your information

Your information is used to operate the platform — to match your wishlist with listings, to enable offers and trades, and to send you relevant notifications. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Contact reveal

Your phone number is only shared with another trader when you have made or accepted a trade offer, consistent with your chosen contact reveal preference. You control this setting in your profile.

4. Rental items and platform liability

Sesika is a marketplace platform only. For rental transactions, the renter is solely responsible for the security, safe return, and any damage, loss, or non-return of rented items. Sesika bears no liability for the outcome of any trade or rental arrangement made through the platform.

5. AI-generated content

Some listing pages display AI-generated context. This content is provided for informational purposes only and may not be accurate. Users can hide or flag AI content on their own listings.

6. Data retention and deletion

You may delete your listings at any time. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at hello@sesika.gh.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: hello@sesika.gh
